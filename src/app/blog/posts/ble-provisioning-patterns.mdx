Key ideas:

- **Ephemeral session key** from ECDH; elevate only after server confirms claim.
- **Out-of-band hint** (QR or short code) to bind app ↔ device.
- **Paperclip reset** clears the *cloud* binding, not just bonds.
- **Log the claim** with a fleet-wide correlation ID.

I prefer a flat protocol—`OP, TLV`—and versioned characteristics, vended under a single primary service.
