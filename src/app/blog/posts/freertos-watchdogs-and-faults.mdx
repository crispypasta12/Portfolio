- Kick the WDG from a low priority **health task**.
- Persist a tiny **fault record** (panic reason, task name, PC) before reset.
- On boot, publish the **reboot reason** once—then clear it.

It’s amazing how quickly field mysteries shrink with three extra keys in your logs.
