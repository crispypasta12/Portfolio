Power budgets look great in a spreadsheet—until you enable timers, DMA and links.  
Here’s the playbook I use on STM32 to _actually_ hit targets:

## Checklist

- **Clocks:** freeze what you can; know which peripherals keep the HSI running.
- **RTC vs LPTIM:** choose your wake source early; it shapes the whole design.
- **DMA + STOP2:** test the exact IPs you’ll use; some combos don’t resume cleanly.
- **Brown-out:** set a real threshold and log *why* you woke up.
